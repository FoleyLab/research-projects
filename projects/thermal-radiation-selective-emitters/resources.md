## Suggested Scientific Questions

* Why does a blackbody obey (T^4) scaling?
* Why does fixing the emission wavelength fundamentally alter the scaling behavior?
* What assumptions are hidden in Stefan-Boltzmann scaling?
* Can highly selective emitters outperform blackbodies at specific wavelengths?
